Monster Concert Location

University Place Mall, 575 East University Parkway, Orem

Date

Saturday, October 31, 2026

Time

There will be four concerts to choose from: 1:00, 2:00, 3:00, or 4:00. All student participants receive a Monster Concert T-shirt.

Group Rehearsals

Rehearsals for all pieces will be held on Thursday, October 29 from 5:00-9:00pm at Brigham Larson Pianos (1497 S State St, Orem, UT). Students will be assigned a 15-minute time window during this time for rehearsal.

Registration

Registration deadline: September 30, 2026

Teachers: Please register yourself first. After you receive your successful registration confirmation, which could take up to 24 hours, you may then send a link to your students to register for the concert of their choice.

Students: Thank you for participating in the 2026 Utah Valley Monster Concert. After your teacher has registered, you may register for the 2026 Monster Concert. The form must be completed and payment received to be registered for the concert.
